Information for Jugglers

Members have recommended the following sites:

  • juggling.se
  • Jack Boyce's JuggleAnim (hosted by CUJA). Good for slowing down a pattern to see what's going on.
  • Diarhythm — Fantastic basic and advanced diabolo trick movies.
  • Home of Poi
  • devilstick.org
  • unicycling.org
